Biryani

Biryani is a mixed rice dish originating among the Muslims of South Asia.
It is made with Indian spices, vegetables, rice, and usually some type of meat (chicken, beef, goat, lamb, prawn, or fish), or in some cases without any meat, and sometimes, in addition, eggs and potatoes

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Basmati Rice
  • Biryani Masala
  • Onions
  • Green Chillies

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat
  2. Add mint leaves, coriander leaves, and green chilies to the pan
  3. Add garam masala, chili powder, carrots, peas and beans
  4. After adding the water, add salt to taste. Stir everything together, then bring the mixture to a boil
  5. Pour the rice into the boiling water. Add the lemon juice. Cover the pot with a tight lid. Let the rice cook until done
  6. Once the rice has cooked, serve the biryani hot
