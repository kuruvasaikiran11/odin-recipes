Chocolate Cake

Chocolate Cake is a cake made with vanilla. It is often covered in icing. It is often eaten at weddings.

Ingredients

  • Instant Chocolate & Vanilla pudding
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Choco Chips

Steps

  1. Pour chocolate syrup into the filled cake pans for an extra chocolate layer
  2. Mix in a handful of chocolate chips for a sweeter flavor
  3. Replace the water with coffee to make the chocolate flavor really pop
  4. Add 1/2 cup of sour cream for an extra moist cake
  5. Substitute butter for the oil to get a richer flavor
  6. Frost the cake with homemade chocolate buttercream
